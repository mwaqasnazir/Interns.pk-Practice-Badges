COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.


The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more sever cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face, and aoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.


How it spreads

Coronaviurs disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a preson toches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose, or mouth.
